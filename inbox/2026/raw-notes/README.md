# inbox/2026/raw-notes

## Purpose
Unstructured, timestamped captures of thoughts, observations, and quick notes.

## What Belongs Here
- Quick markdown notes taken during meetings, incidents, or research.
- Screenshots and snippets with brief context.
- Daily standup or retro notes.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Drafted documents intended for publication.
- Structured project specs or RFCs.

## Lifecycle
1. Write a note with a `YYYY-MM-DD-` prefix.
2. Review weekly; extract action items and knowledge.
3. Move extracted knowledge to `knowledge/`.
4. Delete or archive the raw note once processed.

## Example Entry
- `2026-06-19-api-latency-spike.md`: A one-paragraph observation about a latency spike, including a link to a trace.
