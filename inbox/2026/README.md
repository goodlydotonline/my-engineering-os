# inbox/2026

## Purpose
Staging area for all incoming items in 2026 before they are processed, categorized, and moved to their permanent locations.

## What Belongs Here
- Raw notes, screenshots, links, and files captured during the year.
- Imported data from external sources (e.g., exports, downloads).
- Temporary working files that have not yet been reviewed or classified.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Permanent knowledge or project files.
- Processed, categorized, or archived materials.
- Large binary assets (store those in a dedicated artifact store).

## Lifecycle
1. Items arrive in `raw-notes/` or `imported/`.
2. Review and classify items regularly (e.g., weekly).
3. Move actionable items to `projects/` or `playbooks/`.
4. Move reference material to `knowledge/`.
5. Delete or archive transient items.

## Example Entry
- `raw-notes/2026-06-19-observation.md`: A quick observation about a system behavior during a deploy.
