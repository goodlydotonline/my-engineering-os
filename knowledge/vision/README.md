# knowledge/vision

## Purpose
Reference material on computer vision, image processing, and related ML systems.

## What Belongs Here
- Image preprocessing and augmentation techniques.
- Model architectures for vision tasks (e.g., CNNs, ViTs).
- Evaluation metrics and dataset notes.
- AR/VR measurement and perception workflows.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- General ML theory not specific to vision.
- Raw datasets (store those externally).
- Active project work (use `projects/`).

## Lifecycle
1. Document vision-specific experiments and findings.
2. Distill into reusable guides.
3. Update as the field advances.

## Subdirectories

### `ar-measurement/`
AR-based distance measurement knowledge, primarily ARCore on Android.

- [`ar-measurement/arcore-concepts.md`](ar-measurement/arcore-concepts.md) — ARCore, Depth API, and Android AR hardware support.
- [`ar-measurement/approaches-evolution.md`](ar-measurement/approaches-evolution.md) — Six-stage evolution of distance measurement approaches.
