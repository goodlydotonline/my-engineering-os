# knowledge

## Purpose
Curated, evergreen reference material organized by domain.

## What Belongs Here
- Summaries of technologies, tools, and concepts.
- Lessons learned and best practices.
- Reusable patterns and architectural decisions.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Project-specific docs (use `projects/`).
- Raw, unprocessed notes (use `inbox/`).
- Transient or time-bound information.

## Lifecycle
1. Extract knowledge from `inbox/` and `projects/`.
2. Summarize and generalize.
3. Store in the appropriate domain directory.
4. Review and update periodically to keep evergreen.

## Example Entry
- `backend/api-design-patterns.md`: A distilled guide on REST and GraphQL design patterns.
