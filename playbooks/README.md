# playbooks

## Purpose
Step-by-step procedural guides for common engineering tasks and incidents.

## What Belongs Here
- Debugging procedures and checklists.
- Deployment and rollback runbooks.
- Root cause analysis templates and workflows.
- Project startup checklists.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- General knowledge or theory (use `knowledge/`).
- Project-specific specs (use `projects/`).
- Unprocessed raw notes (use `inbox/`).

## Lifecycle
1. Draft a playbook after encountering or solving a repeatable problem.
2. Review and refine with the team.
3. Update after each use to incorporate new learnings.

## Example Entry
- `debugging/database-lock-contention.md`: A step-by-step guide to diagnose and resolve database lock contention.
