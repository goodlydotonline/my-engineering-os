# roadmap.md

## Purpose
Personal engineering growth roadmap. Defines short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals, plus review cadence to track progress and adjust direction.

---

## Short-Term (0-3 months)

### Goals
- Deepen Kubernetes expertise: finish CKA/CKAD prep and deploy a personal cluster project.
- Strengthen AI workflow skills: build and document 3 agentic workflows using Claude API.
- Improve system design documentation: produce 2 architecture decision records (ADRs) for active projects.

### Success Criteria
- CKA/CKAD practice exam scores consistently above 80%.
- 3 AI workflow playbooks committed to `playbooks/ai-workflow/`.
- 2 ADRs merged into `knowledge/architecture/`.

---

## Medium-Term (3-12 months)

### Goals
- Lead a cross-domain project combining backend, AI, and vision components.
- Establish a reusable post-mortem template and conduct 2 live RCAs.
- Contribute to an open-source tool in the DevOps or AI space.

### Success Criteria
- One shipped project with documented architecture in `projects/`.
- Post-mortem template adopted in `templates/` and 2 RCAs published.
- At least one merged PR or meaningful issue contribution to an open-source repo.

---

## Long-Term (1-2 years)

### Goals
- Become a staff-level individual contributor with recognized expertise in AI-infra systems.
- Build and maintain a public knowledge base (blog or open repo) with 20+ deep-dive articles.
- Mentor others by publishing playbooks and speaking at a technical meetup or conference.

### Success Criteria
- Title or scope change reflecting staff-level responsibilities.
- 20+ published articles linked from `meta/publications.md`.
- At least one talk delivered with slides stored in `meta/talks/`.

---

## Review Cadence

- **Weekly (Sundays)**: Review short-term tasks, update progress, and unblock items.
- **Monthly (last day)**: Assess medium-term milestones, adjust priorities, and archive completed goals.
- **Quarterly (end of quarter)**: Evaluate long-term trajectory, update this roadmap, and set new objectives.

---

## Notes
- Link completed goals to the relevant `projects/` or `knowledge/` directories.
- Use `#learning` and `#verified` tags to track progress in notes.
