# rules/ai-workflow

## Purpose
Definitions, constraints, and guardrails for AI-assisted engineering workflows.

## What Belongs Here
- Workflow definitions for AI-assisted coding, testing, and documentation.
- Constraints and safety guardrails for AI usage.
- Tool use policies and approval workflows.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Individual prompts (use `prompts/`).
- General coding standards (use `coding/`).

## Lifecycle
1. Define workflows based on team AI adoption goals.
2. Enforce guardrails and review AI outputs.
3. Update as AI capabilities and team maturity evolve.

## Example Entry
- `ai-code-review-workflow.md`: A workflow defining when and how AI-assisted code review is conducted.
