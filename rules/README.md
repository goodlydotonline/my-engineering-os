# rules

## Purpose
Prescriptive standards, conventions, and reusable prompts for the engineering team and AI workflows.

## What Belongs Here
- Coding standards and style guides.
- Reusable AI prompts and prompt templates.
- AI workflow definitions and constraints.
- Project-specific rules and guardrails.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- General knowledge or theory (use `knowledge/`).
- Procedural runbooks (use `playbooks/`).

## Lifecycle
1. Propose a new rule based on team consensus or project needs.
2. Document and socialize the rule.
3. Update as tools, languages, or team practices evolve.

## Example Entry
- `coding/go-style-guide.md`: The team's Go coding standards and idioms.
