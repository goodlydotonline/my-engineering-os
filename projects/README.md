# projects

## Purpose
Active and archived engineering work, organized by initiative.

## What Belongs Here
- Project specs, RFCs, design docs, and decision records.
- Runbooks and operational notes specific to a project.
- Post-mortems and retrospectives.

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- General knowledge (use `knowledge/`).
- Generic templates (use `templates/`).
- Unprocessed raw notes (use `inbox/`).

## Lifecycle
1. Create a project directory when a new initiative starts.
2. Accumulate docs, runbooks, and decisions during the project.
3. On project completion or deprecation, move to `archived/`.

## Example Entry
- `archived/2025-payment-gateway/`: A completed project with its final design doc and post-mortem.
